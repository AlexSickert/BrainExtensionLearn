BrainExtension
BrainExtension is an app for learning vocabulary. It makes usage of artificial intelligence to optimize the learning speed.

Easy to use

The application is extremely easy to use and has a simple and clear design that ensures focus and avoid distraction.

See progress

The application shows you in numbers, graphs and tables how your learning is progressing over time and to which extent you forget words.

Built-in translator

When you want to add a new word you can translate it automatically. The translator works with the languages German, English, Russian, Spanish, French, Portuguese, Italian.

Desktop version

You can use the application via mobile app and via desktop in the browser. In both cases you access the same content you entered.

Add several words at once

You can enter words in three ways: Enter word by word, Copy/paste a part of a table from Excel LibreOffice, Google Spreadsheet or use a special Google Spreadsheet that allows to transfer the entire content of the spreadsheet to the app.

Let's get started

To get started please follow these simple steps:

  1. Follow this link in the browser of your mobile phone, notebook or desktop: Link
  2. In your mobile phone add the page to your home screen so that you can easily access it like other apps on your device.
  3. Register with your email address.
  4. Check your email for the password.
  5. Login. You will need to log in only once.
  6. Add words
  7. Learn & enjoy
