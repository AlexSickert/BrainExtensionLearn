BrainExtension
BrainExtension is an app for learning vocabulary. It makes usage of artificial intelligence to optimize the learning speed. It learns the characteristics of the words you most probably forget and forces you to repeat them more often. In addition the application visualizes the learning progress. You can load the vocabulary into the application via Google Spreadsheets.
The application is free of charge, free to use. No strings attached. Sign up here: Start App
Functionality
  • Insert new words that you want to learn
  • Learn the words
  • Upload words via Google Spreadsheet

Roadmap
  • Get statistics about your learning progress
  • Automatically pull text from a URL, translate each word and add it to teh vocabularly list. It ignores words that you already know
  • Paste a text and extract from it all new words
