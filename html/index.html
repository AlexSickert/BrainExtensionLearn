BrainExtension
BrainExtension is an app for learning vocabulary. It makes usage of artificial intelligence to optimize the learning speed. It learns the characteristics of the words you most probably forget and forces you to repeat them more often. In addition the application visualizes the learning progress. You can load the vocabulary into the application via copy/paster from Microsoft Excel and Google Spreadsheets.
The application is free of charge, free to use. No advertising. Sign up here: Start App
Functionality
  • Insert new words that you want to learn
  • Learn the words
  • Upload words via Microsoft Excel and Google Spreadsheets
